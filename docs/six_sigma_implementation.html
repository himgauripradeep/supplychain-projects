🚀 Six Sigma Implementation in Amazon's Supply Chain

📌 Introduction

Six Sigma is a data-driven methodology that improves efficiency, reduces defects, and enhances quality. Amazon applies Six Sigma principles to optimize its supply chain, ensuring faster deliveries, minimal errors, and cost-effective logistics.

1️⃣ Define

Goal: Identify inefficiencies and set operational excellence targets.

  • 🏷 Identifying key supply chain inefficiencies (e.g., delays in order fulfillment).
  • 🎯 Defining customer expectations for fast & accurate deliveries.
  • 📊 Setting data-driven goals for operational excellence.

2️⃣ Measure

Goal: Collect data to monitor supply chain performance.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
  • 📦 Order Defect Rate (ODR): < 1%
  • 📈 On-Time Delivery (OTD): > 95%
  • 📊 Inventory Turnover Ratio: 8x per year
